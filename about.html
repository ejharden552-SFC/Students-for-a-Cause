About Us

Students for a Cause is the student council at Whitman Middle School. We represent the voices of our classmates and create opportunities to make a difference in our school and community. Our mission is simple: to keep Whitman Middle School Better Together.

What We Do

  • Plan School Dances: Bringing fun and excitement to our community through themed dances and social events.
  • Support the Community: Making lunches and collecting donations for families and students in need.
  • Organize School Events: Spirit Weeks, assemblies, and activities that make Whitman Middle School a great place to be.
  • Promote School Unity: Encouraging kindness, leadership, and teamwork with our motto: Better Together.
