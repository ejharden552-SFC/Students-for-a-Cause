Welcome!

Welcome to Students for a Cause, the Whitman Middle School student organisation that makes the school AMAZING. We’re here to make a difference and keep our school Better Together.

🤝 Better Together

We promote kindness, leadership, and teamwork to make our school stronger every day.

We plan fun, themed dances where everyone can relax, enjoy, and celebrate school spirit.

We prepare lunches for Father Bill's homeless shelter as one of the ways we help the community.

📅 Event Planning

From Spirit Weeks to themed days, we organize events that bring Whitman Middle School together.
